Folder Looper Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2025

Your privacy is important. This Privacy Policy explains how Folder Looper ("the App") handles information when you use it.

1. Information We Collect

Folder Looper does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data to the developer or any third parties.

The App does not use analytics, advertising, or any third-party SDKs that collect data.

2. Access to Your Files

Folder Looper allows you to choose a folder using the system file picker.

The App accesses audio files only within the folder you select.

Folder Looper does not access any other files or folders on your device.

Audio files remain on your device and are never transmitted anywhere.

3. Data Security

Since Folder Looper does not collect or transmit any data, there is no data stored on our servers.

All processing happens locally on your device.

4. Your Choices

You control the folders you choose in the App. You can remove access to any folder at any time by selecting a different folder or revoking access in iOS Settings.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
