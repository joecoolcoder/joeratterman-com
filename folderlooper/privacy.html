Folder Looper Privacy Policy

Folder Looper does not require account creation and does not collect personal information.

Information Collection

Folder Looper does not collect, sell, or share personal information.

Files and Audio

Audio files selected through the Files app remain on your device or in the storage location you choose. Folder Looper uses access to play the files you select and does not upload them to a server.
